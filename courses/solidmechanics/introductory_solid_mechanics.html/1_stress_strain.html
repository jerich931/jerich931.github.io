Flux Zero

An original design of a speed Rubikâ€™s Cube for use in official World Cube Association competitions with magnets introduced in the inner radius of the cube to decrease the moment of inertia, reducing the external force required to turn the cube at rates of over ten moves per second. Designed in Onshape and utilized FDM and SLA 3D printing additive manufacturing to produce prototypes and proof of concepts. Magnet technology is currently used in top flagship models for speed cubes on the market and used by tens of thousands of competitive Rubik's Cube speed solvers.
